mirror reaper in ultrakill 8-2 was disappointing

ultrakill's enemy has always felt fair. an enemy could be annoying but that would only be the case because you had not figure out how to deal with that enemy yet. mirror reaper at the end of 8-2? not the case. even the terminal only suggest staying grounded to avoid attacks... you know what? if its ai was a bit more complex then yeah sure i'd accept that as a suggestion. but this thing just runs at you straight. and it's faster than you. similar enemies like the filth work only because, well, filth are easy to kill and do not damage you that much. this just does not work for the mirror reaper. there is nothing you can do. you just slide hopping this thing would not hit you that much (or at least do not inflict any hard damage) as you unload what ever you have.


the mirror reaper fight was a real low point for ultrakill. its a real stain on its squeaky clean record. it actually shat on the extremely unique and interesting rendition of the fraud layer for me. i was really enjoying the awesome arenas that this layer was providing along with the combined challenge the game had been building up to. up until the mirror reaper...
